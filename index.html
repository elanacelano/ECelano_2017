About Me

My Name is Elana Celano and I have been a graphic designer for the past few years. I have my BFA from Tyler School of Art, Temple University. While at Temple I studied abroad for a semester in Rome. Rome was beautiful!

Since my pre-teen years, I always loved to travel. It is so exciting to see a new place with a different culture. Being an artist I thrive on new experiences.

My first big trip was to France. I went to see my best friend who had just moved there, right outside of Paris. I remember spending three weeks with her. I saw Bastille Day which was absolutely magnificent! After that I was hooked. I have been to Puerto Rico with a close friend. I have seen Ireland and Israel. I even made a stop in Russia during a blizzard!
